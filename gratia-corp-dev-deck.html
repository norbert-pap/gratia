Example simulation and evaluation

Sample simulation brief

Scenario: A PE-backed education platform ($100M revenue) is evaluating three add-on acquisition targets in adjacent verticals. You have 3 hours.

Deliverable 1: Screen three targets on strategic fit, financial profile, and integration risk. Recommend one.

Deliverable 2: Build a simple accretion/dilution model with at least two scenarios.

Deliverable 3: Draft a one-page executive memo for the investment committee.

Scoring rubric

Analytical accuracy
Are the numbers right? Does the model hold?
Strategic reasoning
Is the recommendation defensible?
Structure & clarity
Could a VP read this cold and act on it?
Time management
All three deliverables complete within 3 hours?
