The Engagement Framework
Phase 1

Pilot

4 to 8 weeks

A focused engagement where the team sees results before making any long-term decision.

  • One senior analyst, defined scope
  • Clear success criteria agreed upfront
  • Weekly check-ins with team lead
  • No long-term commitment required
Phase 2

Embed

2 to 6 months

The team grows capacity, builds deeper context, and establishes operating rhythms.

  • Add analysts to match workload
  • Pair senior leads with junior support
  • Integrate into team tools and cadences
  • Monthly delivery reviews with stakeholders
Phase 3

Scale

Ongoing partnership

A fully governed program with cross-functional reach, institutional knowledge, and preferred terms.

  • Dedicated account lead and QBRs
  • Cross-team knowledge sharing
  • Preferred pricing tier unlocked at scale
  • Built-in succession planning per workstream

Why a single-partner model delivers more

Zero ramp-up time New teams benefit from institutional knowledge built in other divisions.
Better rates at scale More divisions means preferred pricing for everyone.
One governed process Consistent onboarding, QA, and delivery standards.
Flexible capacity Scale up or down by workstream without hiring overhead.

The Playbook
1

Lunch & Learn: AI, Automation & the Modern Analyst

High Impact

A lunch session for Apollo team leads on how AI and automation are changing the business analyst role. Real examples from active Apollo workstreams show what a modern analyst team actually delivers.

Gives Apollo teams a window into modern analyst capabilities before they ever need to engage.
2

Free 2-Week Trial

High Impact

A new team gets two free weeks of a senior analyst. No paperwork, no commitment. If the team sees value, convert to a paid engagement. If not, no obligation.

Zero paperwork to start. Conversion rate on trials is historically very high.
3

QBR + Adjacent Team Invite

High Impact

At each quarterly business review, invite one lead from an adjacent team. Let them see the reporting, the results, and the operating rhythm firsthand.

Warm exposure in a low-pressure setting. The results speak.
4

Analyst Office Hours

Medium Effort

Weekly open slot where any Apollo team can bring a quick question or small task to a dedicated analyst. Builds awareness, trust, and creates natural entry points for new teams.

Low commitment, high visibility. Seeds future engagements.

The Activation Motion

Identify

Current champion spots the signal
  • Hear a peer mention a capacity gap in a meeting or Teams
  • Notice a team posting for contractors or temp roles
  • Learn about a new initiative without dedicated BA support
  • See a team struggling with data cleanup or reporting backlogs

Introduce

Warm handoff, not a cold pitch
  • Champion sends a 2-line intro: "This works for my team, want to hear about it?"
  • A one-page brief is tailored to the new team's context within days
  • 15-minute scoping call, no commitment
  • Champion stays looped in as a reference throughout

Pilot

Low-risk proof of value
  • One analyst, 4-8 weeks, defined deliverable
  • Weekly check-ins with the new team lead
  • Success criteria agreed upfront
  • No long-term commitment required
