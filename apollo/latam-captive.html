How to use
Salary/mo — What Apollo pays per head at each level. This is the fully loaded monthly salary they see.
Base Cost/mo — Gratia's base salary cost before CLT burden. Editable per level.
CLT burden — Multiplier on base cost for Brazil employment taxes + benefits (13th month, FGTS, INSS, etc). ~1.7× is typical.
Overhead trigger — FTE threshold where EOR, CLT burden, site lead, and office costs kick in. Below it, only base costs apply.
