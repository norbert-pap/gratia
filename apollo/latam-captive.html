Apollo LATAM Captive — Brazil
How to use
Team Composition — Set headcount per Gratia level. Billable and cost rates default to the rate card but can be edited.
Hours/week — Drives monthly fees. 40 = standard, 44 = Brazil CLT.
Discount — Applied to billable rates. Tied to commitment tiers (10 FTEs → 15%, 15 → 20%, 20+ → 25%).
Overhead trigger — FTE threshold where EOR, CLT burden, site lead, and office costs kick in. Below it, only analyst costs apply.
CLT burden — Multiplier on cost rates for Brazil employment taxes + benefits (13th month, FGTS, INSS, etc). ~1.7× is typical.
Ramp Timeline tab — Models 3-month or 6-month ramp from 10 → 20 FTEs using the blended rates from your team mix.
