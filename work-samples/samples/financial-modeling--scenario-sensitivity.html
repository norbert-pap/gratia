Risk Factors

• Customer concentration in top 5 accounts (42% of revenue) creates revenue cliff risk if any large customer churns unexpectedly

• Regulatory changes to payment processor rules could compress take rates by 10–20 bps

• SMB segment declining faster than forecast (22% YoY vs. 25% assumption) suggests market saturation

Recommended Hedges

• Diversify customer base: target 5-year path where top 5 represents <30% of revenue

• Expand platform fees (highest margin, lowest regulation risk) to 50%+ of mix by 2030

• Lock in enterprise contracts with multi-year commitments to reduce churn volatility
