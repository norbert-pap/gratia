Key findings

  • Pipeline is healthy at 3.4× but 62% concentrated in top 3 deals — forecast risk if any slip
  • Forecast accuracy declining (87% → 79% over 4 quarters); commit-to-close slippage averaging 12 days, up from 5
  • 3 deals >$100K stuck >45 days in negotiation need exec sponsor escalation; 2 ramping reps below 40% pace

Recommended actions

1. Deal review on 3 stuck deals — assign exec sponsors. 2. Pair ramping reps with top performers. 3. Require stage-gate evidence for commit category.

Data sources & cadence
