KPI Scorecard Framework: Operations & Growth Alignment

A structured framework for selecting, organizing, and monitoring the metrics that connect operational execution to strategic outcomes — designed for leadership alignment and accountability (illustrative / anonymized).

Key findings

  • Ops efficiency is the strongest quadrant — on-time delivery at 96.2% (above 94% target) for 4th consecutive month; warehouse utilization at 89%
  • Revenue growth flagged amber: pipeline coverage at 2.9× (below 3× threshold); new logo acquisition slowed 18% QoQ
  • Leading indicators predicted churn: support ticket volume in mid-market rose 40% six weeks before logo losses — framework caught it early

Recommended actions

1. Increase pipeline gen — outbound activity 25% below target. 2. Dedicated support for mid-market accounts with ticket spikes. 3. Promote OTD performance in sales narratives to reinforce competitive moat.

Data sources & cadence

Salesforce (revenue), Zendesk (support), WMS (ops), Looker (aggregation). Thresholds calibrated quarterly. Weekly pulse + monthly full review.

Escalation protocol

Amber: Owner investigates within 48 hours, presents root cause at next review. Red: Escalate to leadership within 24 hours with action plan. Two consecutive red readings trigger a dedicated working session.

Implementation note

These thresholds should be loaded into your BI tool (Looker, Tableau, Power BI) as conditional formatting rules. Automated Slack/email alerts on amber/red transitions reduce response lag from days to hours.
