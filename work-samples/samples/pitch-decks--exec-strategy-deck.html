Strategic imperative

Portfolio imbalance threatens profitability. Recommend focused turnaround (optimize low-margin BUs) + growth (adjacent markets) to reach 16%+ EBITDA margin by 2028.

High-level roadmap

6-month diagnostic (Q1-Q2) → 12-month execution (Q3-Q4) → 18-month validation (Y2). Capture $8-12M synergies, unlock $25M growth optionality.

Three Strategic Paths Forward

Optimize

Focus on cost & margin improvements across portfolio.

Pros:

  • Preserve all revenue streams
  • Lower execution risk
  • Improve cash generation immediately

Cons:

  • Limited growth; commoditization risk
  • Difficult talent retention in optimization

Expected EBITDA: $30.8M (15.4%)

+200 bps from portfolio efficiency

Expand

Aggressive growth in Precision + new verticals (aerospace, medical).

Pros:

  • Revenue CAGR 8-10%
  • Strategic optionality (M&A)
  • Talent attraction strong

Cons:

  • Requires $25-35M capex
  • Execution risk (new markets)
  • Margin improvement delayed 2-3 years

Expected EBITDA: $35.2M (15.2% on $232M)

Revenue + margin growth path

Divest

Exit Legacy/Specialty; focus on Ops + Precision core.

Pros:

  • Simplify operations, reduce complexity
  • Margin improves to 18%+ immediately
  • Release $40-50M capital

Cons:

  • Revenue shrinks to $140M
  • Divest execution risk (buyer finding)
  • Smaller platform valuation

Expected EBITDA: $24.9M (17.8% on $140M)

Highest margin, smallest scale

Recommended Path: Optimize + Expand Hybrid
