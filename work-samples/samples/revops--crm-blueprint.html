Target Architecture

System architecture showing data flow from sources through CRM platform to outputs. All integrations API-first.

Sales Stage Pipeline

7-stage funnel with defined entry/exit criteria for each stage. Qualification discipline reduces lead loss.

Stage Conversion % Avg Days Exit Criteria (Move to Next Stage)
Lead 40% 3-5 Email opened + engagement signal OR lead scored 40+ points (meets MQL threshold)
MQL 35% 7-10 Sales rep has contacted; discovery call scheduled OR prospect downloaded high-intent asset
SQL 50% 14-21 Explicit budget/timeline/authority confirmed; fit assessment positive; demo scheduled
Discovery 65% 7-14 Demo completed; detailed notes captured; champion identified; requirements clarified
Proposal 40% 10-14 Written proposal delivered; economic buyer reviewed; pricing discussed; timeline set
Negotiation 75% 5-10 Contract terms finalized; legal sign-off obtained; deal sponsor confident in close date
Closed-Won 100% — Contract fully executed; payment received or on file; onboarding scheduled

Lead Scoring Model

Three-dimensional scoring system: Firmographic (40%), Behavioral (35%), Engagement (25%). Automatically qualifies leads as MQL at 66 points.

Lead Scoring Composition
40% Firmographic
35% Behavioral
25% Engagement
Score Range Grade Stage Action
0–40 Cold Lead (nurture) Email drip campaign; no sales touch
41–65 Warm MQL (sales engaged) Sales rep discovery call within 24 hours
66–85 Hot SQL (qualified) Sales manager involved; demo scheduled
86+ Priority SQL (accelerated) Immediate outreach; executive sponsor loops in
