Key findings

  • Distribution is the moat: Platform-native products win by default placement; challengers must buy distribution.
  • Pricing transparency reduces friction: Complex fees see 15–20% higher "no decision" rates vs. flat-fee.
  • Whitespace: Fast underwriting + transparent terms + partner distribution — no one owns all three.

Recommended actions

1. "Instant approval" flat-fee tier. 2. Shopify Plus agency partnerships. 3. Broaden to "inventory + growth ops."

Public filings, 14 merchant interviews, 8 win/loss reviews, agency calls (Nov–Dec 2025).
