Scoring: Speeds rated by time-to-first-revenue. Costs include sales ramp-up. Risk includes regulatory, integration, market timing.

Recommended sequencing

Hybrid acceleration model

Phase 1 (Now–Q3 2026): Launch organic commercial team; validate product-market fit; pilot partnership with 1 utility.

Phase 2 (Q3 2026–Q2 2027): Scale organic if >$1M ARR run-rate; negotiate acquisition target term sheet; expand partner pipeline to 2–3 utilities.

Phase 3 (Q3 2027+): Integrate acquisition IF internal team already at $2.5M ARR (de-risks overlap). Scale partnerships to 4+ utilities for national footprint.

Why this order wins

  • Organic validates demand + builds domain IP before acquisition.
  • Partnership wins derisks utility regulatory concerns early.
  • Acquisition only proceeds if organic shows traction (lowers integration surprise).
  • Avoids $45–60M spend in unfamiliar market without proof.
