Key findings

• Construction + Prof Services are the focus zone — highest WTP ($150–400/mo) and most urgent triggers

• Home Services has largest base (240K) but lowest WTP; best via PLG where CAC payback is <6 mo

• Triggers cluster around growth events — sales timing should align to project wins, compliance audits, first retainers

Actions

1. Launch Construction first via QuickBooks partnerships 2. Compliance onboarding for Prof Services 3. PLG engine for Home Services volume

Sources

45 interviews, 12 lost-deal post-mortems, 6 focus groups. Van Westendorp pricing + benchmarks vs. Chase Business, Mercury, Brex.
