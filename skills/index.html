Career Progression

Understand the journey from A1 to A4 and what it takes to advance.

A1

Junior Analyst (Apprentice)

Entry Level

  • Learning fundamentals with clear guidance
  • Executes well-defined tasks
  • Needs close supervision
AQS Target: 70-80 Review: Every 3 months
A1 → A2: Building Independence

6-12 months, 10-15 projects

  • 3+ consecutive Gate 2 passes
  • 4+ star client feedback
  • Independence on standard deliverables
A2

Analyst (Independent Contributor)

6-12 months from A1

  • Executes independently on standard deliverables
  • Reliable and accurate
  • Needs guidance on complex situations
AQS Target: 80-90 Review: Every 6 months
A2 → A3: Developing Strategic Thinking

12-24 months, 20-30 projects

  • 5+ projects exceeding Gate 2
  • 4.5+ star client feedback
  • Strategic thinking & sound judgment
A3

Senior Analyst (Strategic Problem-Solver)

12-24 months from A2

  • Designs approach for complex problems
  • Exercises sound judgment
  • Delivers outcomes, not just outputs
  • Mentors junior analysts
AQS Target: 90-95 Review: Annually
A3 → A4: Achieving Mastery

18-36 months, 30+ projects

  • Reference-quality deliverables
  • 5 star client feedback consistently
  • Creates frameworks & trains others
A4

Principal Analyst (Expert & Teacher)

18-36 months from A3

  • Recognized expert
  • Creates knowledge and trains others
  • Handles the hardest problems
  • Sets standards for the team
AQS Target: 95+ Review: Annually
